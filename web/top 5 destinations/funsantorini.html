Ethnic - The Ethnic Cafe Snack Bar is one of the many cafe bars located on the beach promenade that runs along the beautiful beach and it is set against the backdrop of the impressive mountains of Mesa Vouno.

Chilli - Chilli Bar is located on the famous Perivolos Beach of Santorini, which is known for its azure blue waters and tranquil atmosphere. Chilli Bar is one of the hottest destinations on the island for party animals and fun lovers.

Casablanca Soul - Exotic cocktails stirred up by talented bartenders on Santorini. The daquiries, Mojito passion, tropicana and champagne cocktails served at the Casablaca Soul are quite a hit with locals and new comers. George K Tony is the music guru at Casablanca.
