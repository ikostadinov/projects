Hotels

Astartes suites offers a thrilling accommodation experience while spending your holidays on the island of Santorini. It is actually an all suites boutique hotel located in Akrotiri, a beautiful village a few kilometers from the islands capital.

Astro Palace Suites and Spa Hotel is amagnificent resort located in a beautiful part of Fira, the capital of Santorini. It is not far from the towns centre where all the exciting events take place but it is also secluded enough to offer relaxation and serenity.

Carpe Diem Hotel is situated in an ideal location not far from the capital of Santorini. It is also in close proximity to the airport and the harbor of the island.Carpe Diem offers memorable holidays to its guests. The amazing view of the hotel, which is build on top of the hill in Pyrgos village, makes it seem like a palace in a fairytale. Guests of the hotel can enjoy the spectacular view and feel the magic ambience that surrounds the premises of the hotel.
