Restaurants

FANARI RESTAURANT - At the most central part of Fira, at the rim of the caldera, only a few meters from the central square and the cable car! We wait for you here so that you can enjoy traditional Greek cuisine but also the island's unique delicacies, grilled meat, seafood and fresh fish. All these, enjoying the beautiful view of the caldera in a friendly and comfortable environment and in prices that ... don't hurt. The german tour guide ''Santorini Dumont'', recommends our restaurant for its beautiful view, its traditional Greek cuisine and its affordable high quality dishes.

We suggest to make your reservation especially for dinner.

ARCHIPELAGOS - Archipelagos Restaurant, located on the caldera cliffs of Fira town is one of the oldest captain's houses, built in 1860 restored according to the traditional architecture. On its beautiful terraces with view to the volcano, the magical colors of the sunset and the glorious Aegean Sea, you can enjoy lunch or dinner in a romantic environment. Its Mediterranean Greek cuisine, with excellent quality of products, its large selection of Greek wines as well as the professional service will satisfy the most demanding client. In the interior of the restaurant you can enjoy your dinner or lunch in one of the most beautifully decorated places in Santorini, with original art pieces, paintings and sculptures.

Address Calle dello Scaleter - 2202 Venezia
Telephone +39 (0) 41 721308

SPHINX - Restaurant Sphinx has been accommodated in a restored mansion in Fira since 1991. Its unique view over the Caldera of Santorini becomes more breathtaking during the exquisite sunsets just in front of you. For 15 years people from around the world have come to enjoy fine Mediterranean cuisine, an extensive selection of exceptional wines and sophisticated service in the restaurant's relaxing environment. In our menus, we use a wide variety of local ingredients as well as the finest kinds of fresh fish from the Aegean Sea. The extensive wine list contains more than 150 Greek and European wines to accompany your dinner.
