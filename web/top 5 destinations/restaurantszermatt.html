Restaurants

Ristorante Capri - The restaurant on the 4th floor of "Le Petit Cervin" ensures culinary highlights with its excellent Mediterranean cuisine, and is hailed by Gault Millau as the best Italian restaurant in Switzerland. Fantastic views included! (Only open in the winter.)

Speciality: Traditional, Mediterranean cuisine rediscovered.

Reservations: +41 27 966 88 88

Alpenhof Le Gourmet - The Hotel Alpenhof offers a wide range of cuisine at its very best. In the new Zino Platinum Cigar Lounge, you can also crown the delicious Alpine-Mediterranean cuisine by enjoying a fine cigar by the huge open fireplace.

Speciality: Loup de mer (absinthe - Taggiasca olives - fennel) / Lamb filet (Grissini - basil - rye flour gnocchi - peperonata).

Reservations: +41 27 966 55 55
